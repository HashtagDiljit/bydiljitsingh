Pneumatic Piston Model (IDP1 B)

Modelling a Pneumatic Piston with Assembly Drawings

Brief

Design an electric rally pickup platform for Dakar-style conditions with a modular, swappable energy pack.

Scope

  • Vehicle dynamics targets; suspension kinematics; steering geometry.
  • Braking system sizing and thermal considerations; tyre selection rationale.

Highlights

  • Defined roll gradient / ride targets for mixed terrain.
  • Front and rear suspension geometry trade-offs (travel vs packaging).
  • Preliminary brake thermal load model for stage distances.
