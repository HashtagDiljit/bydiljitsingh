Pneumatic Piston — IDP 1A (Fusion 360)

Solo modelling exercise to learn Fusion 360: fully constrained sketches, part modelling, assembly, and a 1:4 drawing pack with clear dimensions, section views and callouts.

Team size: 1 Role: CAD Modelling & Drawings Duration: IDP 1A block Tools: Fusion 360

Context & Challenge

Objective: get comfortable with parametric CAD and drawing standards. I focused on clear diagram layout and adequate dimensioning based on feedback from previous assessments, while avoiding over-dimensioning.

My Role & Learning

Individual project. I iterated from a hand sketch to a clean parametric model, then produced drawings with section cuts and callouts. I asked for feedback on complexity, layout and dimensioning to ensure the drawings met expectations. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
