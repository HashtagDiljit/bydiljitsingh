Wooden Bridge (IDP1)

Cohort-best load test: 2.4 kN (pass: 1.1 kN)

Brief

Design and build a wooden bridge to meet a minimum load requirement with constraints on materials and dimensions.

Approach

  • Selected truss geometry to maximise stiffness-to-mass.
  • Prioritised joint quality and load paths to reduce stress concentrations.
  • Built and tested with a two-person team.

Results

  • Withstood 2.4 kN — highest in cohort.
  • Exceeded pass requirement (1.1 kN) with margin.

Lessons

  • Joint precision and geometry dominate performance in lightweight structures.
  • Lean teamwork enabled faster iteration.
